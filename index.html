About Me

Hey there! I'm Ansaar, a Melbourne-based digital designer specializing in Game Development, Graphic Design, and Web/UI Design. I'm the proud co-founder and manager of my own game development studio, Icebox Games, where I get to bring my interactive & creative visions to life.


My passion for digital design started early on, and I've been dedicated to honing my skills ever since. I earned a certificate in digital media technology and I'm currently pursuing my bachelor's degree at RMIT to stay at the forefront of the industry.


I'm currently working on a relaxing mobile puzzle game called Signals and developing a live sales tracker for Australia's largest telecom company. Exciting projects underway!

02.

The Toolbox

Here are some of the skills, softwares and tools that drive my creations. I'm always working to expand the toolbox, with my next goals being to gain experience and knowledge on the React framework, as well as conducting UI design in Figma as well.


When developing games, I am proficient in Unity 3D as my primary game engine, backed by C-Sharp as the programming language. Majority of the product's assets & visuals are created in Blender . All of my web experiences are drafted in Adobe XD , with all additional visuals created in Illustrator . It is then translated into a HTML and styled with CSS . The website is made functional with my skills in Javascript . Additionally, I utilise Atlassian tools to manage and organise my projects, and am fluent in Premiere Pro to generate video content when marketing them.
